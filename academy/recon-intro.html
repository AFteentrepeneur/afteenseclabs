Module 1: Reconnaissance & OSINT

Before any attack begins, smart hackers do one thing first...

They Recon.

🔍 What is Reconnaissance?

Reconnaissance is the **information gathering phase**. Attackers collect data about a target without touching the system. This phase is silent, legal (if done ethically), and extremely powerful.

🎯 Why Recon Matters

80% of success
Good recon determines most of an attack’s success.
Low risk
Passive recon is usually legal and hard to detect.

Key Skills You Will Master

🌐

Domain & Subdomain Enumeration

Finding all websites and hidden services belonging to a company.

📄

Metadata Extraction

Pulling hidden information from public documents (PDFs, Word files).

👥

Social Media Intelligence

Profiling employees and discovering leaked tech details.
