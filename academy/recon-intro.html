Introduction to Reconnaissance & OSINT

Before any attack, smart hackers do reconnaissance — they gather information about the target without touching anything.

This is called OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence) — using publicly available data from the internet.

Why Recon Matters

  • Finds weak points (emails, employees, subdomains, tech stack)
  • Helps plan phishing or other attacks
  • Ethical hackers use it to find vulnerabilities before criminals do

Basic OSINT Techniques (You’ll Use Today)

  1. Google Dorks — special searches like: site:novamobile.africa filetype:pdf
  2. Social Media Search — Look for employees on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook
  3. WHOIS & DNS Lookup — Who owns the domain? (whois.com)
  4. Company Documents — PDFs often leak names, emails, software versions
  5. Subdomain Tools — crt.sh, dnsdumpster.com (free)
